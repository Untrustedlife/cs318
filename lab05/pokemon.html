Espeon

It evolves from Eevee when leveled up with high friendship during the day or morning, or when leveled up with high friendship with a Sun Shard in the Bag. It is one of Eevee's final forms, the others being Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Glaceon, and Sylveon. It is the starter Pokémon in Pokémon Colosseum alongside the other Generation II Eeveelution, Umbreon.
